# Organizational Practices

## Product Owner for Client Meetings
To ensure all tribe members gain client-facing experience, the Product Owner (PO) role is **rotated on a bi-weekly basis**. 
* Two POs will serve concurrently (one from Team Yellow, one from Team Orange).
* **Current POs (Iteration 0 & 1):** Stanley (Team Yellow) and Abby (Team Orange).
* The active POs are responsible for drafting the meeting agenda, taking minutes, and translating client needs into our GitHub Project backlog.

## Team Coordination within the Tribe
Since Team Yellow and Team Orange are working in parallel on the compass.tne.ai architecture, tight coordination is required to prevent merge conflicts and duplicated work.

* **How issues are raised:** Cross-team issues, API contract disputes, or architectural blockers must be documented as GitHub Issues and tagged with the `needs-tribe-alignment` label.
* **Forum for discussion:** We hold a **Weekly Tribe Sync** (usually immediately following class/lecture time) to review the Kanban board and address blockers. 
* **Required attendance:** The two active Product Owners and at least one technical representative from both Team Yellow and Team Orange must be present. (All members are highly encouraged to attend).

## Team Communication
Our principal method of asynchronous communication is **Discord**. 

* We maintain a dedicated Tribe Discord server with specific channels for `#general-chat`, `#team-yellow`, `#team-orange`, and `#github-alerts` (where PRs and issue updates are piped via webhook).
